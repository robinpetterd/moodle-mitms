Duration is the total length of the training in hours.

For example:

  • "2 hours" is entered as: 2 or 2:00
  • "1 and a half hours" is entered as: 1.5 or 1:30
  • "45 minutes" is entered as: 0.75 or 0:45
  • "20 minutes" is entered as: 0:20

If the training occurs over two or more time periods, the duration is the combined total of the time periods.

This field is optional.
